World Of Awe.The Travelers Journel: Forever

The Purpose

World Of Awe Chapter 1: Forever is a collection of love letters to a different nameless figure, who is frequently referred to as "beloved". These letters in the work express the Traveler's worries, highlighting a greater societal oddity in which the Traveler's past residence in New York stops them from projecting liberal illusions of vitalization onto the Desert, despite the fact that their main purpose is temporary relief.
